About Us


Aim of this project is to design, develop, implement, test and evaluate an effective, secured prototype web application to predict future dam spill over using the rainfall data of the catchment area of reservoir. This prototype web application will also predict the increment of the water level in the dam with the rainfall and this application will be helpful to maintain the water level of the reservoir as well.
